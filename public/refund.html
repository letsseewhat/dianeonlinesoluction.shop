Refund Policy

Last Updated: 2026-07-05

1. Overview

At Diane Online Solution, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not satisfied for any reason, we offer refunds according to the terms outlined below.

2. Refund Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund, you must meet the following conditions:

  • Request a refund within 30 days of purchase
  • Product must be in its original condition
  • All documentation and packaging must be included
  • Valid proof of purchase is provided

3. Refund Process

To request a refund:

  1. Contact our support team at support@dianeonlinesoluction.shop
  2. Provide your order number and reason for refund
  3. We will review your request within 5 business days
  4. Upon approval, we'll provide return shipping instructions
  5. Once received and inspected, refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days

4. Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be issued if:

  • Items show signs of use beyond normal inspection
  • Damage has occurred due to customer mishandling
  • Accessories or documentation is missing

5. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are non-refundable:

  • Digital products or services
  • Personalized or custom orders
  • Items purchased on clearance

6. Refund Method

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used during purchase. Please allow 5-10 business days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your financial institution.

7. Contact Support

For any questions about our refund policy, please contact us at support@dianeonlinesoluction.shop
